About Us - Transcend Next Generation

Our story thus far:

Transcend Next Generation Radios was founded by N4CNR (Rich) with the goal of creating simple, affordable radios for everyone. Our first project is the TrailWave 2MSSB, a 2-meter Single-Side-Band radio (and maybe 6 meters in the future!).
N4CNR is still the lead dev, with the community helping out a lot! Henry (KD9YWF) was brought up to be a leading partner when Rich was facing some medical issues, now, Henry and Rich lead the group together.
We are always looking for more help, so feel free to join our discord and take a look at the github repo for this project.
